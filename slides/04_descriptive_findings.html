Descriptive findings

History

  • Gaming middleware does not derive from theatre show control, nor vice versa — both extend the logic of MIDI (musical instrument digital interface).
  • Some gaming middlewares (FMOD) extend the timeline logic from DAWs (digital audio workstations). Other game audio paradigms (MetaSounds) use flowchart logic.

Required operating concepts

MCP (model context protocol)
A meeting place between the ordinary language an LLM uses and the structured computing languages by which softwares receive API commands.
Middleware
An extensive plugin that connects domains of functionality between softwares.

Problems & solutions

  • Audio-only conveyance of space is difficult. So: speech cues, TTS, and binaural mixing (mimicking pinna filtering for up–down & forward–backward).
  • Binaural mixing is nonstandard across operating systems, and sometimes gated by payware within middleware. Wwise feature licensing is particularly frictious.
