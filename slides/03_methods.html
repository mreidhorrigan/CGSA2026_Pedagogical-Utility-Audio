Methods

1 · Nondeterministic search + prior knowledge

Googling + Claude Opus 4.8 agentic search.

Most important literature: Collins, Game Sound (2008) and Playing with Sound (2013).

2 · Deterministic websearch

Scholarly: OpenAlex. Vernacular: Brave.

Most important literature: the qualitative meta-analysis “Recommendations for the Development of Audiogames,” Teixeira Borges et al. (2017). doi.org/10.1007/978-3-319-58703-5_26

3 · Frontier walkthrough of middleware

FMOD, Wwise, Unity, Unreal MetaSounds, Web Audio API — MCPs for all of them — and Claude Code.

Derived from: “The walkthrough method,” Light et al. (2018). doi.org/10.1177/1461444816675438

But, frontier walkthrough inherits a technoscientific metanarrative of progress (time goes forward) instead of a social platform’s metanarrative of visiting a space (time is cyclical or diffuse). The progress metanarrative fits the concept of project completion—allows a notion of good-enough.
